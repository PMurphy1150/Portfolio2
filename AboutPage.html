ABOUT

Patrick Murphy is a Denver based designer and photographer. With an assortment of work such as printable designs, photography, and physical art pieces. His work often presents itself as gritty, vibrant, and graphic. A variety of work representing urban life, emotion, bike culture, American life, and other community based projects. Murphy looks at it as a goal to design and capture the feeling these daily life experiences thrown at him, while also trying to represent it for others that share the same interest and feeling
